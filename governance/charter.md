# ForgeLink Charter

ForgeLink is the private human contact surface for direct communication, trusted
signals, and agentic systems that need to reach people without becoming a social
feed.

## Principles

1. Private communications outrank novelty, feeds, and engagement mechanics.
2. Humans remain first-class participants; agents request attention, approval,
   and replies through explicit channels.
3. Reliability, recoverable local data, and understandable failure states are
   product features.
4. Secrets, personal messages, media, phone numbers, and agent-human transcripts
   are treated as sensitive data.
5. Work that changes durable behavior leaves durable evidence.

## Non-goals

- ForgeLink is not a social network or attention feed.
- ForgeLink does not optimize for public identity performance.
- ForgeLink does not let untrusted local apps or agents message humans without
  identity, limits, and auditability.
